ABOUT

This is a quick hack to make those background images such as the ones you see on http://www.subtraction.com/2004/12/31/grid-computi and http://www.thegridsystem.org/. I've recently been reading about grid systems in web design, and I'm trying to teach myself the fundamentals. But I discovered that, especially when you want to experiment with lots of variations, drawing such a background image is time-consuming. Or maybe I just have no photoshop skills.

Anyway, I made this tool for myself and those like me, who want an easy way to make grid-system background images.

The grid-maker source code lives on github.

Visit http://xph.us/software to see my other work.
